How to play?
The dynamic is very simple.
Pressing the start button chooses a word at random and the player must guess that word letter by letter.
If the chosen letter is in the word to guess it will appear in the corresponding place of the stripes that were previously placed.
If the chosen letter is not in the word to guess, a part of the hanged man's body will be drawn.
The doll that is being drawn has 6 parts, head, body, two arms and two legs, including the structure where it will be hanged, so the player has 7 options to fail.
